Log Data Backup

Data Backup (Backup)

The key to log management is to preserve past logs using an audit policy and restore them when needed.
Without an audit policy, logs contain no meaningful data.
PLURA uses audit policies to generate and analyze logs to detect anomalies.
All logs that include audit policy context are stored for the long term immediately upon upload and are managed with dual Hot & Cold backups.
